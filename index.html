What this app does

Code & Coin Studio is a personal desktop application used by the owner of the Code & Coin channel to review self-produced educational videos about programming, technology, and personal finance — and to publish approved videos to the owner's own social media accounts.

  • Videos are created and reviewed locally by the account owner.
  • With TikTok's Content Posting API, approved videos are sent to the owner's own TikTok account for final publishing inside the TikTok app.
  • The tool is used by a single person and is not offered as a service to others.
